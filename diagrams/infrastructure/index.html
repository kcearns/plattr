    Domain Pattern¶

    {app}.{baseDomain} → production (prod account) {app}.staging.{baseDomain} → staging (non-prod account) {app}.uat.{baseDomain} → uat (non-prod account) pr-{N}.{app}.preview.{baseDomain} → preview (non-prod account)
